Street Food So Good. Autumn

Street Food So Good — is a weekend food market in Lviv, actually, it’s the first street food festival in the city and just a great platform for supporting local businesses. The main goal of this event is to promote high–quality street food and to develop a new form of outdoor activities that both, citizens and visitors of Lviv will enjoy.

ZaxidFest

All autobahns, air corridors, field trails, forest trails, navigable rivers and even Carpathian fortunes will conspire and all roads will lead not to Rome, but to ZaxidFest in the village of Rodatychi near Lviv! Zaxidfest 2020 is a rock and electronic music festival. Music for a different taste, incredible atmosphere, beautiful Ukrainian nature, friendly smiling people and good memories — this is all Zaxidfest! The festival this year will meet more than 50 famous performers — and this is 60 hours of non-stop music and 500 kW of powerful sound.

Molokiya Lviv Half Marathon

Conquer the streets of Lviv running the Lviv Half Marathon. Overcome your limits together with thousands of like–minded people on a route along the legendary city. Decorated with autumn gold squares and parks, the breadth of history of the city from the start to the finish — this is how our running festival Molokiya Lviv Half Marathon will look like. Join us in August with new strength and goals!

Lviv Coffee Festival

Every year lots of coffee lovers, industry professionals, meticulous roasters, discerning baristas, graceful latte artists and connoisseurs of flavor meet in the city to talk, compete and enjoy this wonderful drink. The event is pursuing the popularization of coffee culture at coffee houses, home, among professionals and coffee lovers.

Lviv Coffee Festival is an event that connects people not only from different cities of Ukraine, but also from different cities of the world. The guests will visit the largest coffee fair that will last for four days of the festival at the old Lviv tram depot. The best coffee shop will be selected during the festival.

Craft Beer & Vynyl Music Festival

Craft Beer & Vinyl Music Festival in Lviv is one of the biggest beer events in Central Eastern Europe. It will gather artists of craft beer culture from Ukraine and worldwide. Various breweries, brewers, pub owners, suppliers and manufacturers of raw materials, equipment, etc. needed for the brewing culture will gather at this festival. A vinyl records exposition of Ukrainian collectors, special equipment and music performed by unique bands and singers will be a pleasant addition to Craft Beer & Vinyl Music Festival.

27 BookForum

If you like reading and books, this event is a must–visit place in your list. 27 BookForum will take place on September 16–20 in Lviv. It is the main culture festival in Ukraine. The honorable guests of 26 BookForum became Indian ecofeminist writer, laureate of Bukers’ award Aruandty Roy; Azerbaijanian writer, autor of the world bestseller “Sweet sault of Bosphor” Elchin Safarli; Hungarian writer, who is one of the top–10 oppositional Russian-speaking writers Maryam Petrosian.

Lviv Cheese and Wine Festival

The main gastronomic autumn fest, Cheese & Wine Festival will take place on the territory of the Potocki Palace and the courtyard of the Palace of Arts. Cheese and Wine Festival is a good opportunity to learn more about the culture of cheese making and winemaking traditions and taste a variety of cheeses and wines. The best products will be presented by well–known producers of Ukrainian wine and cheese. In addition to this, the festival will offer a variety of cheese dishes, including Ukrainian syrnyk. Also, the event's guests will be able to enjoy music performances and different competitions.

Christmas Fair

When the Christmas Fair appears on the main avenue of the city, Lviv begins to feel the true atmosphere of the Christmas and New Year holidays. The fair opens in early December (earlier than in any other corner of the country) and immediately sets the tone of the holiday — the flavors of street candy are spread around the center, bright lights attract both tourists and locals, and such gifts for loved ones as you can get here you will get nowhere else.
