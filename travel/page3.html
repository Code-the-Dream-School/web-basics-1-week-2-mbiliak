Best Places to eat and drink coffee in Lviv

  • Restaurant of Meat and Justice

If you travel to Lviv in order to get unusual experiences and satisfy your taste preferences at the same time, the Restaurant of Meat and Justice is for sure what you have been looking for. According to the legend, the restaurant became the last business of the Lviv executioner, who did not manage to find a job in his field. The restaurant specializes in the preparation of different meat dishes that are cut with the help of medieval tortures tools by the executioner in the flesh.

  • Kryjivka

The most visited restaurant in Europe, 1 000 000 visitors per year. It is the last hiding place of Ukrainian Insurgent Army left from the times of the World War II. Its motto is “The Fight Continues.” When the uniformed guard opens the peep–hole to this theme cafe⁄restaurant and says “Slava Ukraini” (Glory to Ukraine), that’ your cue to respond “Heroyam slava” (Glory to its heroes). This was the greeting used by soldiers of the Ukrainian Insurgent Army (UPA) as they waged guerrilla war against the Nazis, Poles and Soviet Union from 1943–49. It’s also your ticket past the guard. Once inside, you’ll find a fascinating and jovial bunker complete with grenades, rifles and other military antiques. Just as the UPA was immensely popular in Lviv, so is this eatery dedicated to the memory of their sacrifice. Tables can be scarce, so book ahead. Traditional Ukrainian dishes and spirits add to what is certain to be a lasting memory of your visit to Lviv.

  • Arsenal Ribs

You can taste delicious pork ribs and other menu dishes. It’s prohibited to enter wearing a tie! No cutlery — it’s tastier eating with hands! The restaurant is located underground of a historical museum Arsenal. Pork ribs are made on grill stations in front of the guests. As a rule, there are more guests than tables at weekends, thus you can wait and drink beer or juice at a common table.

  • Beer theatre "Pravda"

Beer Theatre “Pravda” is a place where the best world brewers are crafting unique brand of beers, bringing to Lviv their technology and skills. A unique Lviv beer brand is starting history. Their mission is to give people an opportunity to drink real “true” beer. They believe that our city deserves a unique style that is produced only in Lviv and that will be recognized as products as Champagne or Parma. For as little as less than three years beers brewed in Pravda Beer Theatre have collected 14 awards in world beer competitions. The beer at this place is unique because they are named and themed after famous politicians of the world. The tastes and color are associated with famous politicians personalities.

  • Drunk Cherry Bar

Photo by Darya Tryfanava on Unsplash

The best Lviv liqueur is an integral part of old Lviv. Drunk cherry has long been produced in every Lviv house. Every Lviv housewife made such liqueur and every lady knew its taste. The liqueur was so delicious that no woman, regardless of age, could resist its taste. Girlfriends, who got together to have a gossip about the lads, always tasted liqueur. Lviv lads quickly learned that recipe, and for all the dates or strolls, they took a flask of Drunk Cherry. And no lady could refuse.

A glowing red cherry wine shop at Rynok square will surely grab your attention, due to its unique interiors. This stop–over at Drunken Cherry is must Do in Lviv though you don’t drink wine. This cherry wine is a unique and favorite drink of Lviv as they are sourced from locally produced cherries in Carpathian mountains. This cherry wine is amazingly sweet and is not loaded with too much alcohol. It is tasty and jollifies you quickly. It is made based on a traditional Halychyna recipe using the best distillate with selected cherries.

  • House of legends

Photo by Khrystyna Khrun

A restaurant in your imagination only. It is a building of a chimney sweeper and his family where Lviv legends live. The chimney sweeper and his family members collect books about Lviv in their library, protect Lviv lions, watch the water level of the Poltva river and tally if someone has not stolen some old Austrian cobblestone.It is the place where Lviv time is officially protected: the clock hand must not run slow and the clock must keep perfect Lviv time. There is a necessity to clean some chimneys from time to time, so the chimney sweeper takes his car, parked on the rooftop, and passes easily Lviv traffic jams. At night, when the family falls asleep, Lviv smog protects Lviv legends from St. George Hill. The chimney sweeperâ€™s hat has no bottom, but it is connected to a drainpipe. Tourists and visitors of the institution throw coins there. They fall into the chest, and sometimes — on the summer terrace. Therefore, those who decide to drink coffee on the terrace get umbrellas to protect them from the rain of money.

  • Pub–museum “Gas Lamp”

Lviv brought to the world a gas lamp that later on became famous as “Viennese.” Jan Zech and Ignacy Lukasiewicz, the local pharmacists, invented it in 1853. The pub holds one of the biggest collections of gas lamps in Europe. This atmosphere of a real Lviv pub is added by explosions, flashes, smell of gas and rock music. A guide with a lit lantern will lead you up to the restaurant. They serve central European Ukrainian food and for vegetarians, you can order Grilled Vegetables.

  • Lviv coffee mining manufacture

Photo by Khrystyna Khrun

Lviv coffee is born here. It is the only coffee mining where it is extracting underground of Rynok square in front of the very eyes of visitors. Lviv, which is a coffee capital of Ukraine and its coffee houses date back from the 17th century till today. This museum restaurant has unique coffee extraction in the basement. Coffee in Lviv is hidden undergrounds like a mine where miners bring coffee beans through rail track, pulleys to the top and they are roasted, milled and served. You can go down through narrow spiral stairs and see the whole process of mining through iron railings covering basement. After visiting coffee mine, you can enjoy your hot coffee on the Ground floor and also purchase coffee beans.

  • Druzi cafe & bar

Druzi cafe & bar is a network of city cafes for true friends. Here you will find everything to have a great time with your friends and make new acquaintances: board games, funny waiters, good music and the most delicious dishes of the world's cuisine — a great place for you. The motto of our cafe is “Only true friends.” Check it out! It’s like coming to visit your friends who are always happy that you came! The Druzi cafe & bar serves international cuisine, and the menu is updated every season.

  • Cat Cafe Lviv

The first Cat Cafe was opened in lviv in 2015. They really have cats! Here you will get a great opportunity to have a cup of fragrant coffee and spend time in a company of the cutest cats, make great photos for memory and give warmth and attention to our little favorites.If you are a catlover, it is the perfect place for you.

  • Lviv Croissants

Photo by Mariana Vusiatytska on Unsplash

“Lviv Croissants” cafe will fascinate you with cozy and positive atmosphere. They use only natural ingredients and offer more than 30 types of croissants. Salty and sweet, as well as, the opportunity to choose the additional ingredients are offered to guests.

They bake our croissants directly in cafe and cook it in front of the guests after guests make an order, which guarantees not only freshness, but also gives the croissants an extraordinary taste. Cafe also offers a large assortment of hot and cold drinks of own cooking. Cozy atmosphere and modern interior allows you not only to enjoy delicious meals, but also to hold meetings⁄negotiations or relax from a hard day.

  • Little Green

In a hurry, but want to eat delicious? Here you are! Little Green — fast and healthy meal. You can find vegetarian and vegan dishes in the menu, as well as sweets of home cooking.

  • Svit Kavy

If you are looking for locals’ all–favorite place to sit around and watch the life pass by, go straight to the charming little coffee shop right opposite to the Boim’s Chapel and Latin Cathedral. Svit Kavy or the World of Coffee is famous for its beautiful interior, lovely summer terrace and, of course, more than 30 varieties of coffee to taste! Deliciously brewed, it will take you to the core of what Lviv is all about. Order up traditional Lviv cheesecake and you can call yourself the real Lviv–er.
